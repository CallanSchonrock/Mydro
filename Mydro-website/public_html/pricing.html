Mydro Licensing Options

Mydro can be downloaded for free and is throttled to single simulations. Mydro has Yearly and Monthly subscription options.

Unlimited Mydro has functionality for:

  • Batch processing
  • Ranked Ensembles
  • Event based storm losses

Monthly

100$AUD / Month

  • Suitable for one-off projects
  • As there is a free version of Mydro it is usually recommended to trial the free version of software before consideration of upgrading

Yearly

1,000$AUD / Year

  • Suitable for repeat modelling purposes
  • Save a little $$$ on a yearly subscription
